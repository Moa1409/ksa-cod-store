# 01 — Brand & Positioning

## Brand
- **Arabic name:** لمسة توهج
- **English name:** Lamsa Glow
- **Meaning:** *Lamsa* = "a touch"; *توهج/Glow* = radiance. → "a touch of glow."
- **Domain:** `lamsaglow.shop` · **Support email:** `care@lamsaglow.shop`
- **One-line promise (AR):** «صالونكِ الخاص… بلمسة توهج، في بيتكِ.»

## Category design (don't compete — own a category)
We do **not** sell "beauty gadgets." We create and own:
**«العيادة الجمالية المنزلية» — The At-Home Beauty Clinic.**
Competitors sell a cheap *device*; Lamsa Glow sells a **clinic-grade beauty ritual you own, in private, forever.** This reframes:
- **Price** → vs years of salon bills (value, not cost).
- **Trust** → clinic/certified tech, not a random gadget.
- **Identity** → a woman who invests in herself.

## Positioning statement
> For the modern Saudi woman who wants to look effortlessly radiant but is tired of the cost, wait, and lost privacy of salons — **Lamsa Glow** is the at-home beauty clinic delivering salon-grade results with clinical-grade, **certified** technology. Unlike cheap online gadgets, every Lamsa Glow device is safety-certified (CE/RoHS), engineered with **named technologies**, and backed by a results guarantee, COD, and thousands of Saudi women.

## The 4 brand pillars (every page must reinforce all 4)
1. **نتائج الصالون** — Salon-grade results (proof it works).
2. **تقنية موثوقة ومعتمدة** — Certified, safe technology (science + certificates).
3. **خصوصية وراحة** — Privacy & convenience (home, on her time, modest).
4. **قيمة تدوم** — Value that lasts (one purchase vs endless salon bills).

## Unfair angle (the KSA cultural insight)
At-home beauty devices uniquely solve **privacy + modesty + convenience + repeat-cost** for Saudi women. She can style hair, remove hair, and treat skin **in complete privacy at home** — no appointments, no exposure, no recurring bills. We own this narrative.

## Brand voice
- **"أختك الخبيرة" (your expert big-sister):** warm, confident, caring, precise. Gulf/KSA dialect.
- **Aspirational & empowering**, never body-shaming (ad-policy safe): *"تستاهلين تحسّين بجمالك"* — never *"عالجي عيبك"*.
- Confident but honest: real proof, "النتائج تختلف من شخص لآخر" where needed.

## Emotional territory we own
Confidence, radiance, self-care, control, privacy, "treat yourself," being put-together effortlessly.

## What we avoid (ad compliance)
- No medical claims / no before-after in TikTok ads / no weight-loss / no shaming.
- Cosmetic framing only. Add "results may vary" + shipping/origin disclosures where required.
