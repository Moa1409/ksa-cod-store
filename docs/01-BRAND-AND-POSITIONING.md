# 01 — Brand & Positioning (Lamsa Glow · Certified Salon Rituals)

## Brand
- **Arabic name:** لمسة توهج
- **English name:** Lamsa Glow
- **Meaning:** *Lamsa* = "a touch"; *توهج/Glow* = radiance. → "a touch of glow."
- **Domain:** `lamsaglow.shop` · **Support email:** `care@lamsaglow.shop`
- **One-line promise (AR):** «صالونكِ الخاص… بلمسة توهج، في بيتكِ.»

## Category we own
We do **not** sell random beauty gadgets or commodity AliExpress jars. We own:

**«طقوس الصالون المعتمدة في بيتكِ» — Certified At-Home Salon Rituals.**

Competitors sell a cheap mask or mist; Lamsa Glow sells a **certified salon ritual** (repair → freshness → inner strength) with Halal + ISO 22716 / GMP proof, COD, and loyalty reorder.

## Positioning statement
> For the modern Saudi woman (22–45) whose hair feels damaged, frizzy, or flat under the hijab — and who is tired of salon bills and uncertified online products — **Lamsa Glow** is the certified at-home salon ritual brand that restores softness, freshness, and confidence with keratin science, Halal authority, and cash-on-delivery trust. Unlike cheap no-name serums, every Lamsa Glow product shows real certificates, named ingredients, and social proof from Saudi women.

## The 4 brand pillars (every page reinforces all 4)
1. **نتائج الصالون** — Visible softness / freshness / strength (proof it works).
2. **حلال ومعتمد** — Halal · ISO 22716 / GMPC · GMP · COA (authority).
3. **خصوصية وراحة** — Home ritual, on her time, modest.
4. **قيمة وولاء** — Better than endless salon visits + monthly reorder stack.

## Unfair angle (KSA cultural insight)
Covered hair + heat + color + social occasions = a **daily emotional problem** (frizz, dullness, odor anxiety) that salon visits only temporarily fix. We own: **certified ritual under the hijab**, not another “hair loss cure.”

## Brand voice
- **"أختك الخبيرة"** — warm Gulf dialect, confident, never shaming.
- Aspirational: *"تستاهلين تحسّين بجمالك"* — never *"عالجي عيبك"*.
- Honest: no medical claims; "النتائج تختلف من شخص لآخر".

## Emotional territory
Confidence · radiance · put-together · freshness before going out · control · smart self-investment.

## What we avoid
- Medical / hair-loss / whitening / weight / intimate claims
- Fake SFDA “approved” badges without registration
- Drinks/tea, stick-on patches, uncertified electronics without QC
