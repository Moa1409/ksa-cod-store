## Category we own

We do **not** sell random TikTok jars or “miracle” gummies.

We own:

### «الصيدلية التجميلية المعتمدة في بيتكِ»
**Certified At-Home Dermocosmetic Parapharmacy**

A curated care ritual of:
1. **Topical care** — keratin/collagen mask, hair perfume mist (ISO 22716 / GMPC / MSDS / COA)
2. **Inner beauty support** — collagen · keratin · biotin gummies (GMP / ISO 22000 / HACCP / COA)

Same shelf logic as a French-style parapharmacy: **clear active ingredients · recognized certifications (ISO / GMP) · honest claims · reorder ritual** — not medical treatment, not cheap dropship aesthetics.

---

## Positioning statement

> For the modern Saudi woman (22–45) who wants salon-level softness and freshness under the hijab — and daily beauty support she can trust — but refuses uncertified online products and endless salon bills, **Lamsa Glow** is the **certified dermocosmetic parapharmacy at home**. We combine clearly named active ingredients, recognized certifications (ISO / GMP / COA / MSDS), Gulf-warm expertise, and cash-on-delivery so she confirms, receives, and reorders with pride.

**Vs. salon:** same desire for put-together hair — owned as a private home ritual, better long-term value.  
**Vs. cheap TikTok beauty:** certificates + ingredient transparency + COD risk reversal.  
**Vs. pharmacy coldness:** warm “أختك الخبيرة” voice, feminine confidence, hijab-relevant rituals.

## The 4 brand pillars

1. **معايير الصيدلية التجميلية** — ISO 22716 / GMPC / GMP / COA / MSDS before she pays.
2. **مكوّنات فعّالة مكتوبة بوضوح** — keratin · collagen · argan · biotin — named on the page, explained honestly.
3. **عناية بخصوصيتكِ** — under the hijab, at home, on her time.
4. **ثقة عند الباب** — COD · inspect before pay · 30-day guarantee · reorder loyalty.

## Brand voice

- **«أختك الخبيرة في الصيدلية التجميلية»** — warm Gulf dialect + calm authority.
- Aspirational: *تستاهلين عناية واضحة* — never *عالجي عيبك*.
- Honest: «النتائج تختلف» · «مو علاج طبي» · show only certificates we hold.

## Emotional territory
Confidence · put-together · freshness before going out · smart self-care · trust · control.

## What we avoid
- Fake SFDA “approved” badges without registration  
- Medical / hair-loss / whitening / weight claims  
- Halal badge without certificate on file  
- Neon hype, scammy urgency, AliExpress-looking packaging language

## Color system (parapharmacy clinical-beauty)

Signature: **Deep Teal** = trust / authority / primary CTA.  
**Forest Ink** = headings & footer.  
**Soft Brass** = ratings / certificate accents.  
**Sage Mist** = soft fills.  
**Cool Pearl** = page background (not warm terracotta cream).
