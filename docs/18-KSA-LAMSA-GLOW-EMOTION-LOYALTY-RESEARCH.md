## 2. Selection filter (what “good” means for you)

### Brand fit (Lamsa Glow pillars)
1. **نتائج الصالون** — visible salon-like result  
2. **تقنية / اعتماد** — Halal · ISO 22716 / GMPC · COA (devices: CE/RoHS)  
3. **خصوصية وراحة** — home ritual, modest, no salon exposure  
4. **قيمة تدوم** — one purchase vs endless salon bills; then **repeat SKUs** for LTV  

### Emotion engine (from `docs/02-ICP`)
| Pain | Unspoken emotion | Desire we sell |
|---|---|---|
| شعر مجعّد / باهت / ما ينتظم | إحباط، “ما أطلع حلوة مثلهم” | ثقة + إطلالة مرتبة بسهولة |
| ريحة / تفلّت تحت الحجاب | خجل اجتماعي قبل المناسبة | انتعاش + فخر عند الباب |
| بشرة باهتة قبل المناسبة | “مو جاهزة” | إشراقة سريعة |
| فواتير صالون ما تنتهي | ذنب من الصرف | استثمار ذكي في البيت |

### COD confirm + delivery (from STRATEGY + Exa)
| Maximizes confirm/delivery | Kills it |
|---|---|
| Real need + visible result | Pure fad / shame product |
| Pride at doorstep (premium jar, scent, gift look) | Looks cheap / “scam serum” |
| Trust badges (Halal, ISO22716, COD, guarantee) | Fake SFDA claims |
| Event / routine deadline | No urgency after click |
| Beauty consumables RTO often **&lt; 5%** when quality + certs | Fashion / intimate / weight / hair-loss cures |

**WhatsApp confirm &lt; 5 min** remains the #1 operational lever (60–70% → 85–92%).

### Economics
- Source Alibaba OEM under ~**$10 landed** where possible  
- Retail **129–249 SAR** consumables · **299+ SAR** devices later  
- Margin funds ads + SFDA cosmetic listing + next SKU  

### Explicitly excluded (your prior rules)
Drinks / tea / anything to ingest at launch · stick-on face patches · body whitening · oversold hair-loss serums · medical claims · sexual products · uncertified cheap electronics without QC

## 4. Product 1 — HERO ⭐  
## «لمسة جلو — ماسك الكيراتين لإصلاح الروابط»

### Big problem (audience size)
Saudi women 22–45 with **heat / color / friction under hijab** → dry, frizzy, broken ends, “ما ينسرّح.” Nexdigm lists damage repair, dryness, frizz, and hijab-related needs as core demand states.

### Emotion → urge to buy
- **Pain:** “شعري باين إنه متعب… ما يطلع مثل الصالون.”  
- **Emotion:** frustration + comparison shame (TikTok/Snap looks).  
- **Urge:** “أبغى حل الليلة / قبل المناسبة — صالون في البيت.”  
- **Ad hook:** before-event + after-one-wash softness (cosmetic framing only).

### Why confirm + delivery stay high
| Factor | Effect |
|---|---|
| Heavy salon jar + cert card | Pride at door — not “cheap serum scam” |
| Visible result after 1 wash | She still wants it when courier calls |
| Not hair-loss / not medical | Low embarrassment refusal |
| Price 199–249 SAR | Commitment filters joke orders |
| Halal + ISO 22716 on PDP + WhatsApp | Trust for first-time COD |

### Loyalty / scale
- Use **2–3×/week** → empty jar in **4–8 weeks** → reorder  
- Upsell mist → clay → later AirGlow device  
- Line extensions: travel sachet, “intensive” 500ml, bride kit
