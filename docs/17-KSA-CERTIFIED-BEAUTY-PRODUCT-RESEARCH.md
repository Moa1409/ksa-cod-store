## 2. What KSA women actually look for (certification hierarchy)

Research sources: SFDA cosmetic guidance, ChemLinked KSA regulations, Malak Beauty / Gomyz halal positioning, IndexBox KSA supplements report, NIQ Beauty Decoded KSA 2026, your `docs/03-TRUST-AUTHORITY-PROOF.md`.

### Tier 1 — Must show on PDP & WhatsApp (confirmation infrastructure)

| Badge (AR) | What it means to her | COD impact |
|---|---|---|
| **حلال 100%** | Safe for faith + family — not “random Chinese powder” | ↑ Confirm · ↑ Delivery |
| **ISO 22716 / GMPC** | Factory makes cosmetics to international GMP (GSO 2020) | ↑ Trust at doorstep |
| **COA + MSDS متوفرة** | Batch quality + safety data — “clinic-grade seriousness” | ↓ “scam” fear |
| **مصنع معتمد** (not fake SFDA product claim) | Professional origin story | ↑ Loyalty |

### Tier 2 — Scaling & legal (plan before volume)

| Requirement | Applies to | Cost reality |
|---|---|---|
| **SFDA cosmetic notification (GHAD / eCosma)** | Hair mask, hair mist, clay mask | Listing process; notifier holds responsibility for safety |
| **SFDA food supplement registration (FRCS)** | Gummies | **~5,000 SAR new product** + GMP/HACCP/ISO22000 docs — **not a $500 launch item** |
| **FASEH Certificate of Conformity (CoC)** | Import clearance at port | Needed when you import bulk yourself |
| **Halal cert** | All products | Not legally required for cosmetics, but **strongly drives purchase** in KSA |

### Tier 3 — Electronics only

| Badge | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **CE + RoHS** | EU safety baseline — show on PDP for hot-air tools |
| **FCC** | If supplier exports to US — extra credibility |

### Critical honesty (do not fake this)

1. **SFDA cosmetic “listing” ≠ SFDA approved formula.** SFDA states notification creates a database; **notifier is responsible for safety** (SFDA Guidance Requirements Cosmetic Product Notification).
2. **You cannot print “مرخص من SFDA” on gummies** until food supplement registration is complete — only registration reference number on label (SFDA Registration Guide Food Supplements).
3. **Factory Halal cert ≠ your brand is halal** until your SKU is covered — get certificate scoped to your product/batch or manufacturer letter.
4. **$500 budget:** Launch with **Tier 1 supplier certificates on site + WhatsApp** → fund SFDA paths from first profits.

**Reference KSA DTC doing this well:** [Malak Beauty](https://malak.beauty/) leads with **Halal · SFDA · COD · 30-day guarantee** — certification + risk reversal in one fold.

## 9. Supplier vetting checklist (before you pay)

Ask every factory:

1. Send **Halal certificate PDF** — issue date, scope, cert body name  
2. Send **ISO 22716 or GMPC certificate** — match factory name on Alibaba  
3. Send **COA + MSDS** for the exact formula you will sell  
4. Send **CPSR** (cosmetics) if available  
5. Confirm **ingredient list** matches your Arabic label (no banned SFDA ingredients)  
6. **Video of production line** or third-party audit report (optional but powerful for ads)  
7. Written MOQ for **50–100 trial** with custom box  

**Red flags:** Refuses certificates · Only sends logo mockups · Halal cert expired · ISO cert factory name ≠ supplier name
