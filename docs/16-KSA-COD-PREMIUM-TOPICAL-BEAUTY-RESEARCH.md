## 2. What you taught us (and what we killed)

### You were right to reject
| Rejected | Why |
|---|---|
| Ampoule/vial serums | Look cheap/OEM; hard to premiumize at your MOQ |
| Drinks / tea / collagen latte | You don't want ingestible liquids |
| Stick-on patches (acne, eye, collagen patches) | You said no "stick on face" |
| Body whitening | Low COD confirm/delivery + shame |
| Rosemary/biotin **hair loss serums** | Oversold → confirm crash |
| Generic slimming collagen coffee | Weight-loss ad trap + oversold |
| Cheap LED/jade gadgets | Low trust, weak loyalty |

### What actually wins on KSA COD (your rules + data)
1. **Big problem × big audience** (hair damage, dull skin, hijab hair freshness)
2. **Emotional pain** (impulse) + **daily ritual** (intent survives 5–10 day shipping)
3. **Doorstep pride** — looks like Nice One / salon product, not AliExpress bag
4. **Repeat purchase** — jar empties, mist runs out, 30-day gummy cycle
5. **Ad-safe framing** — glow, repair, freshness, strength — never "cure baldness" or "whiten body"
